## ✨ Key Features
BeatFlow takes your local music listening experience to the next level with a clean, ad-free UI and powerful features:
* 📂 **Folder Browsing:** Play music directly from your device's storage folders.
* ❤️ **Favorites System:** Mark your top tracks and access them instantly.
* 🙈 **Hide Songs:** Keep unwanted audio (voice notes, ringtones) out of your library.
* 🗑️ **Delete Tracks:** Permanently delete unwanted files directly from the app.
* ⏱️ **Sleep Timer:** Fall asleep to your favorite tunes; the app pauses automatically.
* 🔔 **In-App Updates:** Get notified automatically when a new version is released.
* 🎨 **Material You Design:** Beautiful dynamic colors that match your phone's theme.
* ⚡ **Highly Optimized:** Extremely small APK size for fast performance.
